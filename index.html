We derived this idea in September 2013 with my friend Jason in BA3200 at the University of Toronto. We've noticed there is a mobile plane battle game in Wechat has become dramatically popular over the past two months in China. It was like a recreation for the classic 2D game in mobile version with social element added in. After getting addicted into this game and "waste" our time day and night, we decided to make our own version in HTML5, for fun and for practice. We have another technical developer Andrew, and we have an amazing graphic designer Ryoga All of those fabulous pictures were painted in hand.

We use the name "Attack on Plane" in regards to the hot Japanese comic "Attack on Titans"

We created the explosion sprite based on the knowledge from this tutorial http://www.gamingthinktank.com/2012/12/07/animating-walk-cycle-in-html5-canvas-using-kineticjs-javascript-framework/

And the picture source is here http://members.gamedev.net/dfgames/explosion.png
